In which cases a refund applies:

Sponsorship payment via PayPal, Patreon on the DigitalPlat sponsorship page (Hack Club Bank is strictly monitored by the bank, once the payment is successful, the fund will only be used for non-profit organizations, so it is non-refundable)

Within 1 hour, this is the cooling-off period for refunds. If you meet the refund requirements, you can apply for a refund of the full amount, and you do not need to pay handling fees (except handling fees charged by the platform or other necessary fees).

Please note that there are only two free refunds per donor, and a 5% service fee will be charged for more than two refunds.

For refunds between 1-12 hours, you will incur an additional 5% manual refund service fee, applicable to any sponsorship amount.

No refund will be given after 12 hours

 

 

How refunds are processed and when:

Attach a screenshot/picture of successful payment (you can submit the order number or payment code attached to some payment methods), and provide payment user name, ID and other information to ensure a smooth refund. Send these payment information to service@digitalplat.org with the subject: <Donation Refund>, please add the above attachments required for the refund, and send the reason for the refund. Generally, refund requests will be processed within 72 hours.

The timing of the refund time starts from the moment you successfully send the email

We cannot guarantee that your request for a refund will be 100% successful, so please think twice before donating.

If you donate to us, you agree to our refund policy.
